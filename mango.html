THE FRESH AND ORGANIC MANGOES

Mango is my favorite fruit. The reason I love mangoes so much is their lip-smacking flavor and crazy driving fragrance. In India there are over 100 mango varieties found. The size, color and flavor of all of them are different. I love the 'Hapus ' mangoes most of all of these varieties. They're luscious and sweet.

Alphanso

Alphonso is also one of the most expensive varieties of mango, and is grown mainly in the Konkan region of western India.The famous Alphonso mango or Hapus is produced in Maharashtra's Ratnagiri, Raigad, and Konkan regions.Ratnagiri Alphonso Mango is well recognized due to its unique taste, aroma, and superior quality. The size of these mangoes is about 4-6 inches.

Price

1200/Dozen

Kesar

Is Kesar variety of mango? Kesar Mangoes is the highest exported mango variety from India. It is loved for its luscious taste and extreme sweetness. While Hapus (Alphonso) is called the King of the Indian fruit, Kesar is commonly also called the Queen.

Price

800/Dozen

Payri

Payri is an extremely delicate mango with low shelf life. Typically it is juiced immediately when ripe and the juice is stored (refrigerated or frozen) for future use. The season for Payri is generally the second half of the month of May.ango Pairi tastes like pure honey with a blend of sweetness and sourness. The pulp is fibrous and juicy. The skin is yellowish green with a reddish tinge.

Price

1000/Dozen
